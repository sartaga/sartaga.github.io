The planning team determines the development sequence and sets initial completion dates for each feature set and major feature set.<br/><br/><br/>The Design Team Business Analyst will assemble all existing requirements, wireframes, and business rules and produce a Feature Spec Document from the finalized feature list. The Feature Spec will contain all information required by the Development Feature Team to design, build, and code the feature as well as all information required for the Test team to test the feature. Test Cases and Outcomes, Translations, Screen mockups, and business rules should be contained within the Feature Spec. Michelle can provide examples of the format for these specs from My Social Center.<br/>All requirements, test cases, test plans, and feature specs that are going to be used in production systems must be checked into the TFS architecture project under the Requirements folder, for version control purposes and to provide a permanent traceable record of requirements. SharePoint can also be used to share documents with the business but the system of record for requirements is the requirements folder in TFS.<br/>
